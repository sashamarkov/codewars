<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/69d1b72f7b4d8ac09b1eb17a/train/javascript target="_blank">Raffle Run: What Are the Odds?</a></h2><h3>7 kyu</h3><h1 id="raffle-run-what-are-the-odds">Raffle Run: What Are the Odds?</h1><p>You've entered multiple independent raffles back to back. Each raffle is a single-winner draw: one ticket is pulled from the pool, and if it's yours, you win.</p><p>Given two arrays of equal length:</p><ul><li><code>totals</code> — the total number of tickets sold in each raffle</li><li><code>purchased</code> — how many tickets you bought in each raffle</li></ul><p>Return your probability of winning <strong>at least one</strong> raffle as a <strong>simplified fraction string</strong> in the form <code>"numerator/denominator"</code>. This fraction must be full reduced, so "4/8" becomes "1/2" and "9/12" becomes "3/4." </p><hr><h2 id="probability-hint">Probability Hint</h2><p>For independent events, the probability of winning at least one is the complement of losing all of them:</p><blockquote><p><strong>P(A or B or ...) = 1 - P(!A) * P(!B) * ...</strong></p></blockquote><p>where <strong>P(!X)</strong> is the probability of <em>not</em> winning raffle <strong>X</strong>.</p><hr><h2 id="examples">Examples</h2><pre><code class="language-javascript"><span class="cm-comment">// One raffle, 1 ticket out of 3</span><span class="cm-variable">raffleOdds</span>([<span class="cm-number">3</span>], [<span class="cm-number">1</span>])           <span class="cm-comment">// =&gt; "1/3"</span><span class="cm-comment">// Two raffles, 1 ticket each out of 4</span><span class="cm-variable">raffleOdds</span>([<span class="cm-number">4</span>, <span class="cm-number">4</span>], [<span class="cm-number">1</span>, <span class="cm-number">1</span>])    <span class="cm-comment">// =&gt; "7/16"</span><span class="cm-comment">// Three raffles</span><span class="cm-variable">raffleOdds</span>([<span class="cm-number">2</span>, <span class="cm-number">3</span>, <span class="cm-number">6</span>], [<span class="cm-number">1</span>, <span class="cm-number">1</span>, <span class="cm-number">1</span>])  <span class="cm-comment">// =&gt; "13/18"</span></code></pre><hr><h2 id="notes">Notes</h2><ul><li>All values are positive integers</li><li><code>purchased[i] &lt;= totals[i]</code></li><li>If you've purchased no tickets in any given raffle, that raffle has no impact on your odds </li><li>If you are guaranteed to win any single raffle, return <code>"1/1"</code></li><li>The returned fraction must be fully reduced</li></ul>

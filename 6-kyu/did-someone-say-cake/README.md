<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/564e624da64546f72e0000e8/train/javascript target="_blank">Did someone say cake?</a></h2><h3>6 kyu</h3><p>Given the below recipe for chocolate cake write a function <code>cake()</code> that takes two inputs: <code>ingredient</code> and <code>amount</code>. </p><p>The recipe should be adjusted according to the amount passed into the function. An <code>Object</code> containing all ingredients and their new amounts should be returned.</p><p>Example: <br>If I have just 80g of caster sugar, how much will I need of the other ingredients?</p><pre><code class="language-javascript"><span class="cm-variable">cake</span>(<span class="cm-string">'caster sugar'</span>,<span class="cm-number">80</span>) <span class="cm-operator">=&gt;</span> {<span class="cm-string">'caster sugar'</span>: <span class="cm-string">'80g'</span>, <span class="cm-variable">butter</span>: <span class="cm-string">'85g'</span>, <span class="cm-variable">eggs</span>: <span class="cm-number">1.5</span>, <span class="cm-string">'self-raising flour'</span>: <span class="cm-string">'57.5g'</span>, <span class="cm-string">'cocoa powder'</span>: <span class="cm-string">'27.5g'</span>}</code></pre><p>Note that the new amounts should be rounded to 1 decimal place and unit of measurement for <code>amount</code> will always be in grams, unless the <code>ingredient</code> is eggs. </p><p>Here is the original recipe:</p><pre><code>160g caster sugar170g butter3 eggs115g self-raising flour55g cocoa powder</code></pre><p>This is given in the initial solution.</p>

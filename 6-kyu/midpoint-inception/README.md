<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/56d67c7eecb553574900016c/train/javascript target="_blank">Midpoint inception</a></h2><h3>6 kyu</h3><p>Write</p><pre><code class="language-javascript"><span class="cm-keyword">function</span> <span class="cm-def">midPoint</span>(<span class="cm-def">str</span>)</code></pre><p>that takes all the words in a string <code>str</code> and cumulatively appends their midpoints<br><br>For example:</p><pre><code class="language-javascript"><span class="cm-variable">midPoint</span>(<span class="cm-string">'abc'</span>) <span class="cm-operator">===</span> <span class="cm-string">'b'</span><span class="cm-variable">midPoint</span>(<span class="cm-string">'abcd'</span>) <span class="cm-operator">===</span> <span class="cm-string">'bc'</span> <span class="cm-comment">// when length is even, then midpoint is 2 chars</span><span class="cm-variable">midPoint</span>(<span class="cm-string">'abcdefg'</span>) <span class="cm-operator">===</span> <span class="cm-string">'dbf'</span> <span class="cm-comment">// 'd' is the midpoint of 'abcdefg' (abc)d(efg) --&gt; go left split first</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'b' is the midpoint 'abc'</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'f' is the midpoint of 'efg'</span><span class="cm-variable">midPoint</span>(<span class="cm-string">'abcdefgh'</span>) <span class="cm-operator">===</span> <span class="cm-string">'debg'</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'de' is midpoint of 'abcdefgh' (abc)de(fgh)</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'b' is midpoint of 'abc'</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'g' is midpoint of 'fgh'</span><span class="cm-variable">midPoint</span>(<span class="cm-string">'abcdefghij1234567890'</span>) <span class="cm-operator">===</span> <span class="cm-string">'j1ebcgh63489'</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'j1' is midpoint of 'abcdefghij1234567890' (abcdefghi)j1(234567890)</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'e' is midpoint of 'abcdefghi' (abcd)e(fghi)</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'bc' is midpoint of 'abcd' (a)bc(d)</span><span class="cm-comment">// 'gh' is midpoint of 'fghi' (f)gh(i)</span><span class="cm-comment">// '6' is midpoint of '234567890' (2345)6(7890)</span><span class="cm-comment">// '34' is midpoint of '2345' (2)34(5)</span><span class="cm-comment">// '89' is midpoint of '7890' (7)89(0)</span><span class="cm-variable">midPoint</span>(<span class="cm-string">'abc def'</span>) <span class="cm-operator">===</span> <span class="cm-string">'b e'</span> <span class="cm-comment">// words can be space seperated</span><span class="cm-variable">midPoint</span>(<span class="cm-string">'a ab abc abcd abcde abcdef abcdefg abcdefgh abcdefghi abcdefghij'</span>) <span class="cm-operator">===</span> <span class="cm-string">'  b bc c cd dbf debg ebcgh efbchi'</span></code></pre><p>Notes:</p><ul><li>Always go left and then right when finding midpoints</li><li>Keep all the whitespaces of the original sentence</li><li>A "word", here is a sequence of chars delimited by whitespaces or beginning or end of string.</li></ul>

<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/596f1bdeda9b3b0297000018/train/javascript target="_blank">Simple Fun #344: Children And Apples</a></h2><h3>6 kyu</h3><h1 id="task">Task</h1><p>Here are some children, each with a certain number of apples. Now we have to do something to make the number of apples of each child are equal.</p><p>We need to complete the redistribution step by step. With each step, we can transfer <code>two apples</code> from one child to another (whether or not adjacent does not matter).</p><p>Given an array <code>apples</code> that represents the number of apples per child. Your task is to calculate the minimum step of the transition operation. If it's impossible, return <code>-1</code>.</p><h1 id="example">Example</h1><p>For <code>apples=[7,15,9,5]</code>, the output should be <code>3</code>.</p><pre><code>                       7 15 9 5step 1: 15-2, 5+2 ---&gt; 7 13 9 7step 2: 13-2, 7+2 ---&gt; 9 11 9 7step 3: 11-2, 7+2 ---&gt; 9  9 9 9</code></pre><p>For <code>apples=[7,7,7,7]</code>, the output should be <code>0</code>.</p><p>The children's apples are equal already. No need more step.</p><p>For <code>apples=[7,7,7,5]</code>, the output should be <code>-1</code>.</p><p>26 apples can't be divided equally between 4 children.</p>
